About Us

Established in 2022, Boulder Dash is quickly becoming Limerick's most popular climbing club. This is a club specific to those interested in indoor and outdoor bouldering. The club is open to people of all ages with an under 12 club occuring on Sunday morning. Check out our website and join the community to keep up to date with the latest information from the club
